ReptileWiki

ReptileWiki is a web-based app/ social media platform that tracks the details and history of individual reptiles so that potential buyers can be informed of the condition of the specific reptile that they are buying. Users can search for a specific reptile or no reptile in particular, and view that reptile's history. Users can also share their collection on their profile for others to view, and post status updates to their profile that other users can read. This can be updates about reptiles, information or care advice, or something else.

Function of the App

This app aims to fulfill the following:

Guaranteeing the Condition of a Reptile

Options when acquiring a reptile include:

  • Buying from a shop, such as Pets at Home or a local reptile shop
  • Buying from a breeder online, or at a reptile expo
  • Rescuing or adopting a reptile from a previous owner

All 3 of these options, especially the last 2, do not guarantee the condition of the reptile that is being bought. It could have underlying health issues, been kept in unsuitable conditions, or could have been imported rather than captive bred. This app documents the owner history and health history of the reptile so that the condition can be guaranteed when buying. The history of a reptile could include:

  • Origin of the reptile (Store-bought, captive-bred, wild-caught, details of the breeder)
  • All previous owners
  • Previous vet visits
  • Previous/current health issues
  • Previous/current medication

The owner would be responsible for updating the history of the reptile.

Ensuring Trustworthiness of a Breeder

When buying a reptile from a breeder, the term 'reputable' is thrown around. Ensuring a breeder is reputable is completely up the the buyer. This is a problem because the reputation of the breeder is often only understood within the reptile community, and a first-time buyer may not have the same knowledge and understanding as members of this community. This app aims to verify breeders based on reviews by other users, and their activity within the app.

Following Users/ Sharing Personal Collections

Each user can have a collection of reptiles they own, available for anyone to view on their profile. This could be like an individual's page on Instagram. Each individual reptile would have a history, some general information about that reptile, and any updates the owner has posted that are about that reptile.
